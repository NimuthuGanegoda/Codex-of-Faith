# Christianity: The Faith of the Risen Christ

## Core Beliefs
Christianity is a monotheistic religion based on the life and teachings of Jesus of Nazareth.
* **The Trinity:** Belief in one God existing in three persons: The Father, The Son (Jesus Christ), and The Holy Spirit.
* **Jesus Christ:** Believed to be the Son of God, fully divine and fully human, the Messiah promised in the Old Testament.
* **Salvation:** The belief that humanity can be reconciled with God through faith in Jesus' sacrifice on the cross.
* **The Resurrection:** The belief that Jesus rose from the dead, proving victory over sin and death.

## The Sacred Text
* **The Bible:** Consists of the **Old Testament** (Jewish scriptures) and the **New Testament** (the Gospels, letters of the Apostles, and early church history).

## Basic Practices
* **The Great Commandment:** To love God and love one's neighbor.
* **The Ten Commandments:** Moral laws provided by God.
* **Sacraments:** Rituals such as **Baptism** and **Communion (Eucharist)**.
* **Holy Days:** **Christmas** (Jesus' birth) and **Easter** (Jesus' resurrection).

## History and Branches
* **Origins:** Emerged in the 1st century AD as a Jewish sect in the Roman Empire.
* **Major Branches:**
    * **Roman Catholicism:** Led by the Pope; emphasizes tradition and sacraments.
    * **Eastern Orthodoxy:** Emphasizes ancient liturgy and icons.
    * **Protestantism:** Focuses on the Bible as the sole authority and salvation by faith.
